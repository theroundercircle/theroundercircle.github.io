Meet the Band

The Rounder Circle is a bluegrass/americana band from the SF Bay Area. Formed in 2024 by current members Hilary Nelson, Thomas Hatcher, Ilan Ladabaum, and Kyla Kent, TRC's sound is rooted in strong instrumentals, tight vocal harmonies and original songwriting.

Their upcoming debut album, California Bound, is a collection of diverse yet cohesive original songs (plus one cover!), ranging from odes to California to songs about small-town gossip. Follow along as the album is released! New single every 3 weeks, with the full album dropping this June!
